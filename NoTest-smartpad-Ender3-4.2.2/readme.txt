Klipper firmware CREALITY-4.2.2-4.2.7.bin
Version: v0.13.0-693-g5ac7c301 (built 2026-07-17, Yumi-Lab fork, post-build verified)

WARNING: this machine profile is marked NoTest — never validated on a
real machine. Flash at your own risk and test carefully.

TARGET: Creality 4.2.2 / 4.2.7 board (STM32F103, USART1 PA10/PA9 @250000, 28KiB).
FLASH: rename to a UNIQUE name ending .bin, different from the last one flashed
(e.g. firmware-20260717.bin), copy at the ROOT of a FAT32 SD card, insert in
the printer SD slot, power-cycle, wait ~30s.
Older builds are archived in the dated subfolders.
