Klipper firmware ANYCUBIC-VYPER.bin
Version: v0.13.0-693-g5ac7c301 (built 2026-07-17, Yumi-Lab fork, post-build verified)

TARGET: Anycubic Vyper mainboard (TriGorilla, GD32F103 clone, USART1 PA10/PA9
@250000, 32KiB bootloader).
FLASH: rename to main_board_20xxxxxx.bin (the digits must DIFFER from the last
flash), copy at the ROOT of a FAT32 SD card, power OFF, insert, power ON.
Older builds are archived in the dated subfolders.
